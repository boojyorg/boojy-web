Boojy is a solo project built by Tyr. I care about your privacy and want to be transparent about what data is collected.

Website

Analytics

This website uses Umami, a privacy-focused analytics tool:

  • No cookies
  • No cross-site tracking
  • No personal information collected
  • Only anonymous data: pages visited, browser type, country

Email Signup

When you subscribe to updates, we collect your email address only. Managed via Mailchimp. We will never sell or share your email.

Boojy Audio

Local Storage

All projects and settings are stored locally on your computer. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud.

Crash Reports (Opt-in)

You can choose to send anonymous crash reports. These include:

  • Error type and stack trace
  • OS and app version
  • No personal data, project files, or audio

This is opt-in only — you choose on first launch.

Update Checker

The app can check for updates. This only sends your current version to see if a newer one exists.

Boojy Notes

Local Storage

Notes are stored locally in your browser. If you use Boojy Cloud, notes are synced to our servers (encrypted in transit and at rest).

Boojy Cloud

If you sign up for Boojy Cloud, we collect your email address and store your synced notes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We do not read, analyze, or share your notes.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You can:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of emails anytime

Email tyr@boojy.org to exercise these rights.

Data Sharing

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone.
