Hey — I'm Tyr. I'm starting a short monthly note here on what's changed across Boojy. Nothing
fancy: just a calm record of what got better, so you don't have to dig through release pages to
keep up.

This month the big one is **Boojy Cloud**. Your notes now sync across your devices for free, up to
500 MB — no account juggling, no subscription. It quietly powers Boojy Notes today, and a paid tier
with more room is on the way (with Boojy Audio support to follow).

I also gave **boojy.org** itself a fresh coat — a clearer homepage, an honest picture of where each
app is, and this page you're reading. Audio and Notes are both still in early access and moving
quickly; I'll keep these notes coming as things land.

Thanks for being here this early. If something's broken or you've got an idea, the feedback box on
the home page comes straight to me.
